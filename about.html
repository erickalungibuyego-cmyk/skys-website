Skys High Schools

Skys High Schools are two branches currently, Skys High Kampala-Ndejje started in 2018 with about 100 students which was one of the greatest archievements to the founder. By the end of 2018 schools went on a lockdown due to the COVID-19 pademic . Recently in 2024 December, the founder and Director Mr. BUYEGO ERICK with Management opened up a new Campus, Skys High Mityana-Lubanja.

The Schools are operating on the New curiculum setting as the MINISTRY OF EDUCATION & SPORTS organised.

Our passion with Sports has helped us to attract many students from different region of the country and outside the country.We have countinued to participated in the PPS games all time round.
Netball is also another game that is mostly liked by mostly girls and some few boys, this has promoted unity.
